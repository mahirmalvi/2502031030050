Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is delivery?
Our average delivery time is under 10 minutes from the time you place your order. We operate dark stores close to residential areas to make this possible.
What is the minimum order value?
There is no minimum order value! However, orders below ₹499 will incur a small delivery fee of ₹49. Orders above ₹499 get free delivery.
Can I return an item?
Yes! We have an easy 24-hour return policy. If you're unhappy with any item, contact us and we'll arrange a pickup and refund within 48 hours.
Which payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major UPI apps (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), debit and credit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery.
Is the app available on iOS and Android?
Yes! EasyMart is available on both the App Store and Google Play. You can also shop directly from our website.
